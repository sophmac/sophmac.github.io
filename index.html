Academic bio

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at the University of British Columbia (UBC), under the supervision of Brian Marcus and Tom Meyerovitch. My research is in symbolic dynamics. I finished my Master's (MSc) in Mathematics in August 2019, also in the UBC Mathematics Department, under the supervision of Gordon Slade. Before coming to UBC, I finished my Hons Bsc in Mathematics and Statistics at McMaster University in April 2017. My undergraduate research advisers were Bartek Protas, Walter Craig (now sadly deceased), and Masoud Khalkhali (through the Fields Institute's Undergraduate Summer Research Program).

I have worked extensively as a teaching assistant in the Mathematics Department, as well as in the Master of Data Science program. I also facilitate workshops on teaching and presentation skills through UBC's Centre for Teaching, Learning, and Technology (CTLT), and I have co-facilitated the Math Department's TA training since 2020. Currently, I am a mentor in the Research Experience Program at UBC.
